# 📊 Northwind Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Business Objective

Analyze sales data from the Northwind database to identify key revenue drivers, top-performing products, and customer purchasing patterns. The goal is to generate actionable insights that can help improve sales strategy and customer retention.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* SQL (PostgreSQL / MySQL compatible)
* Northwind Database
* GitHub for version control

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The Northwind database is a sample dataset that simulates a company's sales operations, including:

* Customers
* Orders
* Order Details
* Products
* Employees
* Categories

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

The following analyses were conducted using SQL queries:

### 1. Revenue Analysis

* Total revenue generated
* Revenue by month and year
* Identification of sales trends over time

### 2. Product Performance

* Top-selling products by revenue
* Most frequently purchased products
* Category-level performance

### 3. Customer Analysis

* Top customers by total spend
* Purchase frequency
* Identification of high-value customers

### 4. Employee Performance

* Sales generated per employee
* Comparison of performance across sales representatives

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* A small percentage of products contributes to the majority of revenue (Pareto principle).
* Certain customers consistently generate high revenue, indicating opportunities for loyalty programs.
* Sales show seasonal patterns, suggesting potential for targeted marketing campaigns.
* Some employees outperform others significantly, highlighting best practices worth replicating.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products to maximize ROI.
* Implement retention strategies for high-value customers.
* Use seasonal trends to plan promotions and inventory.
* Analyze top-performing employees’ strategies to improve overall team performance.
